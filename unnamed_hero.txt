
Level 3 CLERIC
====================

Attributes and Statistics
-------------------------
Hit Points:       13
Armor Class:      3
Damage Reduction: 1
Fighting Ability: 2
Experience      : 8000

Strength:      12
Dexterity:     15
Constitution:  15
Intelligence:  13
Wisdom:        16 +10% XP bonus
Charisma:      14

Saving Throw: 15, Death +2, Sorcery +2
Background skill: Messenger

Gear
----
banded mail, spear, light crossbow (w/20 bolts), torches (x3),
backpack, wineskin, bandages, tinderbox, trail rations (1 week), 12
gp, horn, incendiary oil, glue

Class Features:
---------------

*Clerical Sorcery*
Through prayer, you can invoke the power of your deity to perform miracles,
inscribe spells upon holy scrolls (cost of 500 gp + 100 gp/spell level),

*Turn Undead*
Exert control over undead or demonic beings.

Spells:
-------
Serpent Charm, Enthral, Find Traps, Augury, Distinguish Alignment,
Fire Resistance, Prayer, Remove Paralysis, Animate Dead
