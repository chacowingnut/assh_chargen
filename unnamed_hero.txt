
Level 3 CLERIC
====================

Attributes and Statistics
-------------------------
Hit Points:       14
Armor Class:      6
Damage Reduction: 1
Fighting Ability: 2
Experience      : 8000

Strength:      13
Dexterity:     13
Constitution:  11
Intelligence:  10
Wisdom:        14
Charisma:      11

Saving Throw: 15, Death +2, Sorcery +2
Background: Common cartwright

Gear
----
scale, scimitar, light crossbow (w/20 bolts), torches (x3), backpack,
wineskin, bandages, tinderbox, trail rations (1 week), 6 gp, ink and
quill, collapsable 10-ft pole, wooden stakes (x4)

Class Features:
---------------

*Clerical Sorcery*
Through prayer, you can invoke the power of your deity to perform miracles,
inscribe spells upon holy scrolls (cost of 500 gp + 100 gp/spell level),

*Turn Undead*
Exert control over undead or demonic beings.

Spells:
-------
Detect Silence, Darkness, Serpent Charm, Incantation, Enthral,
Invisibility to Undead, Continuous Light, Dispel Magic, Meld into
Stone
