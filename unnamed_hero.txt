====================

Attributes and Statistics
-------------------------

Armor Class:      10
Damage Reduction: 0
Fighting Ability: 0
Experience      : 0

Strength:      12
Dexterity:     6
Constitution:  9
Intelligence:  10
Wisdom:        12

Saving Throw: 16, Device +2, Sorcery +2
Background: Common mason

Gear
----
dagger, darts, torches (x3), backpack, wineskin, bandages, tinderbox,
trail rations (1 week), 9 gp, soap, block of beeswax, spool of wire

Class Features:
---------------

*Magician's Familiar*
Summon a small animal (1d3+1 hp). You can see through its eyes as long as
it is within 120 ft. If it dies, you lose 1 hp per level.

*Sorcery*
Ability to cast spells memorized from an arcane tome. You can also decipher
otherwise unintelligible magical inscriptions or symbols, inscribe spells
upon magical scrolls (requires 500 gp + 100 gp/spell level), and brew magical
potions.

Spells:
-------
Magic Missile, Spider Climb, Burning Hands, Identify
