
Level 6 THIEF
====================

Attributes and Statistics
-------------------------
Hit Points:       23
Armor Class:      8
Damage Reduction: 0
Fighting Ability: 4
Experience      : 24000

Strength:      13
Dexterity:     12
Constitution:  11
Intelligence:  13
Wisdom:        12
Charisma:      11

Saving Throw: 14, Device +2, Avoidance +2
Background: Common bookbinder

Gear
----
padded, dagger, darts, lamp (w/full flask of oil), backpack, wineskin,
bandages, tinderbox, trail rations (1 week), 6 gp, spool of wire,
block of beeswax, marbles

Class Features:
---------------

*Agile*
-1 AC when unarmoured and unencumbered.

*Backstab*
Sneaky attacks have a bonus to hit probability and damage.

*Thieves' Cant*
You speak the secret language of criminals.

*Thief Skills*
You are proficient in all manner of skullduggery, specifically: climbing,
decipherment, careful listening, hiding, trap manipulation, moving silently,
lock picking, pick pocketing, and reading magical scrolls.
